# Cookie & Local Storage Policy

**Redemption OS**  
**Effective Date:** June 25, 2026

---

Redemption OS uses **browser local storage** (not traditional cookies) to maintain your session. This is strictly necessary for the app to function.

## What We Store Locally

| Key | Purpose | Expiry |
|-----|---------|--------|
| `redemption-session-storage` | Maintains your active event session | Until logout or browser clear |
| `redemption_mock_session` | Legacy demo session (no longer used in production) | Until cleared |
| Firebase Auth tokens | Maintains your logged-in state | Auto-managed by Firebase SDK |

## What We Do NOT Use

- ❌ Advertising cookies
- ❌ Third-party tracking cookies
- ❌ Analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics)
- ❌ Cross-site tracking

## How to Clear

You can clear local storage at any time by:
- Logging out of Redemption OS (clears session data)
- Clearing your browser's local storage via browser settings
