These documents are designed to address:

- ✅ **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation — EU)
- ✅ **NDPR** (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation)
- ✅ **COPPA** (Children's Online Privacy Protection — US)
- ✅ **Standard app store legal requirements** (Google Play / Apple App Store)

## Before Going Live — Legal Checklist

- [ ] Replace placeholder email addresses (privacy@, data@, legal@, abuse@) with real ones
- [ ] Have the documents reviewed by a qualified legal professional in your jurisdiction
- [ ] Add links to Privacy Policy and Terms of Use on the login/register pages in the app
- [ ] Implement a consent checkbox on the registration page
- [ ] Set up a process to respond to data subject requests within 30 days
- [ ] Ensure `serviceAccountKey.json` is never committed to your git repository
