## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account information:** Full name, email address, password (encrypted), phone number
- **Profile information:** Profile photo, role selection, address, emergency contact details
- **Family member information:** Names, photos, dates of birth, medical/allergy information of registered children or family members
- **Communications:** Messages sent within the platform, incident reports, broadcast content

### 3.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage data:** Pages visited, features used, actions taken within the app
- **Device information:** Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Log data:** IP addresses, timestamps, error reports
- **Performance data:** App crash reports and error logs (via Sentry)

### 3.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Firebase (Google):** Authentication data and database storage
- **Cloudinary:** Media files you upload (images, videos) are stored on Cloudinary's servers
- **Sentry:** Error and performance monitoring data

---

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide the Service** — Create and manage your account; enable login; display your profile
- **Child Safety & QR Identity** — Generate and manage QR codes linked to family member profiles for safety check-in/check-out during events
- **Communication** — Deliver broadcasts, messages, and notifications relevant to your role
- **Marketplace** — Process orders, manage vendor listings, and coordinate deliveries
- **Incident Management** — Enable community signal reporting and operations coordination
- **Security** — Protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and abuse
- **Improvements** — Analyze usage patterns to improve features and performance
- **Error Monitoring** — Diagnose and fix technical issues using crash reports

## 5. How We Share Your Information

We do **not** sell your personal information. We share data only in the following circumstances:

### 5.1 With Service Providers (Data Processors)

| Provider | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|----------|---------|----------------|
| **Google Firebase** | Authentication, database, hosting | https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy |

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests from public authorities.

## 6. Children's Privacy

### 6.1 Users Under 18

Redemption OS is intended for use by adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

### 6.2 Family Member Profiles (Children)

Parents and guardians may register minor children as **family members** within the platform. This information (name, photo, medical notes) is used solely for:
- Event safety and QR-based check-in/check-out
- Emergency contact purposes during the event

This information is:
- Accessible only to the registering parent/guardian and authorized event security staff
- Not shared with third parties
- Deleted upon account deletion or upon request

---

## 7. Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored on **Google Firebase** servers (Firestore and Firebase Authentication)
- Media files are stored on **Cloudinary's** secure servers
- Data is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS
- Passwords are hashed using Firebase Authentication's industry-standard encryption
- We implement access controls so users only see data relevant to their role
- We monitor for errors and security incidents via Sentry

Despite these measures, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
